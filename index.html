References for 17th century paints

The pigment checker is an attempt to create a reference collection of 17th century Dutch paint samples by the Technical Art History master's at the University of Amsterdam.

Because mixing with lead white was common at the time, the second square shows what each paint looks like mixed with 50% v/v lead white paint. and their hiding power.

Colour

The painted squares can be used to examine the colour that specific paints produce when mixed with linseed oil.
